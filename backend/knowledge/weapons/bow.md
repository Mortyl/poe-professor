# Bow

## Weapon-Skill Restriction
Skills below can **only** be used when a Bow is equipped. Do not suggest these skills for any other weapon type.

## Tags Reference
Tags determine what passive tree nodes and support gems affect a skill. Tags in brackets after each skill name.These are used to scale the damage or mechanics of the skill.

---

## Active Skills

### Primary Damage
- **Lightning Arrow** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Area · Lightning)* — fires a charged arrow that chains lightning to nearby enemies
- **Rain of Arrows** *(Attack · Ranged · Area · Physical)* — fire a hail of arrows into the air that rains down over a target area
- **Poisonburst Arrow** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Area · Chaos · Duration)* — fires a virulent arrow that creates a burst of Poison on hit
- **Detonating Arrow** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Fire · Channel · Area)* — channel then release a burning arrow that detonates; strong Ignite builds
- **Tornado Shot** *(Attack · Ranged · Area · Physical · DoT · Duration)* — fires a shot that spawns a Tornado dealing Physical damage over time
- **Snipe** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Channel · Cold · Area)* — channel to charge up before releasing an extremely powerful single shot
- **Ice Shot** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Cold)* — fires an icy arrow that sprays a cone of ice shards on hit
- **Shockchain Arrow** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Lightning)* — fires a Lightning-charged arrow that homes in on Shocked enemies
- **Electrocuting Arrow** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Area · Lightning)* — fires an arrow that explodes in an area on hit
- **Spiral Volley** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Area)* — fire enchanted arrows in a circle as you spin forward
- **Barrage** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile)* — empowers your next bow attack to repeat multiple times; pairs with other bow skills

### Utility / Setup
- **Gas Arrow** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Area · Chaos · Duration)* — fire a noxious arrow creating a cloud of poisonous flammable gas
- **Vine Arrow** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Area · Duration)* — fire an arrow that causes a plant to spring up, blocking enemies
- **Stormcaller Arrow** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Lightning · Duration)* — arrow embeds and periodically calls down lightning strikes
- **Phantasmal Arrow** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Duration)* — arrow embeds where it lands (setup tool)
- **Escape Shot** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Cold · Travel)* — leap backwards while firing an icy arrow
- **Lightning Rod** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Lightning · Area)* — fire an arrow that drops from above creating a Lightning burst
- **Freezing Salvo** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Cold · Sustained)* — passively coalesces icy missiles over time
- **Ice-Tipped Arrows** — empowers your next bow attacks to convert Physical Damage to Cold
- **Toxic Growth** *(Attack · Ranged · Area · Chaos · Duration)* — fire a rain of Toxic Pustules into the air
- **Toxic Domain** *(Area · Chaos · Duration)* — create an area of Toxic Bloom on the ground around you

### Persistent Buffs
- **Mirage Archer** — while active, dodge rolling creates a Mirage that uses your socketed ranged attacks
- **Wind Dancer** — periodically grants stages of a buff giving more Evasion per stage

### Heralds *(usable with any weapon)*
- **Herald of Thunder** *(Lightning)*, **Herald of Ice** *(Cold)*, **Herald of Ash** *(Fire)*, **Herald of Blood** *(Physical)*, **Herald of Plague** *(Chaos)*

## Key Tag Interactions
- **Attack** — scaled by Attack Damage, Attack Speed, and Accuracy nodes on the passive tree
- **Ranged** — scaled by Ranged Attack nodes
- **Projectile** — scaled by Projectile Damage and Projectile Speed nodes
- **Area** — scaled by Area of Effect and Area Damage nodes
- **Lightning/Cold/Fire/Chaos** — scaled by the matching elemental damage nodes; determines which ailments are applied (Shock, Freeze, Ignite, Poison)
- **DoT** — scaled by Damage over Time nodes, not hit damage
- **Channel** — affected by Channelling nodes; cannot be supported by Cast Speed

---

## Playstyle Notes
Bow builds are primarily ranged, relying on positioning. Lightning Arrow is the dominant clear skill in EA. Detonating Arrow is the go-to for Ignite builds. Snipe is best for Crit-focused single-target. Bow attacks scale with Projectile, Attack Speed, and elemental nodes on the passive tree.
