# Quarterstaff

## Weapon-Skill Restriction
Skills below can **only** be used when a Quarterstaff is equipped. Do not suggest these skills for any other weapon type.

*Note: Path of Building labels these skills as "Staff" weapon type internally, but they are Quarterstaff skills in PoE2.*

## Tags Reference
Tags determine what passive tree nodes and support gems affect a skill.

---

## Active Skills

### Primary Damage — Lightning
- **Tempest Flurry** *(Attack · Melee · Lightning · Area)* — series of aggressive Strikes; primary fast-hitting Quarterstaff skill
- **Storm Wave** *(Attack · Melee · Area · Lightning)* — sweep Quarterstaff projecting a Lightning bolt fissure along the ground
- **Gathering Storm** *(Attack · Melee · Channel · Lightning · Area)* — flip backward and channel Lightning before unleashing
- **Charged Staff** *(Attack · Melee · Lightning · Duration · Buff)* — consume Power Charges to add Lightning damage and shockwaves to attacks
- **Vaulting Impact** *(Attack · Melee · Slam · Area · Physical)* — leap forward and Slam the ground sending out a shockwave

### Primary Damage — Cold
- **Ice Strike** *(Attack · Melee · Cold · Area)* — fast icy Strike; core Cold Quarterstaff skill
- **Wave of Frost** *(Attack · Melee · Area · Cold · Travel)* — flip backwards and send forth a Freezing wave
- **Glacial Cascade** *(Attack · Melee · Area · Cold)* — sweep Quarterstaff releasing an icy fissure culminating in a large spike
- **Frozen Locus** *(Attack · Melee · Cold · Area · Duration)* — leap backward and summon an Ice Crystal

### Mobility / Gap Close
- **Flicker Strike** *(Attack · Melee · Area)* — teleport to an enemy and Strike; consumes Power Charges
- **Hand of Chayula** *(Attack · Melee · Travel)* — dash to an enemy, triggering socketed Marks and applying socketed Curses
- **Killing Palm** *(Attack · Melee · Travel)* — dash to an enemy and Strike; Culls enemies below Life threshold
- **Shattering Palm** *(Attack · Melee · Cold · Area · Travel)* — dash to an enemy and Strike, manifesting a wave of cold
- **Staggering Palm** *(Attack · Melee · Travel)* — dash to an enemy and Strike; Heavy Stuns enemies Primed for Stun

### Combo Builders
- **Tempest Bell** *(Spell · Duration)* — build Combo by Striking enemies; releases a burst when struck
- **Mantra of Destruction** *(Buff · Duration)* — build Combo by Striking enemies; empowers subsequent attacks
- **Whirling Assault** *(Attack · Melee · Area · Physical · Travel)* — advance while hitting enemies with a series of spinning Strikes

### Utility
- **Wind Blast** *(Attack · Melee · Area · Physical)* — batter enemies at range by generating a gust of wind
- **Charged Shockwave** *(Attack · Melee · Area · Lightning)* — release a Lightning-charged shockwave
- **Siphoning Strike** *(Attack · Melee · Travel)* — dash to a target and Strike; drains a resource

### Persistent Buffs
- **Wind Dancer** — periodically grants Evasion stages

### Heralds *(usable with any weapon)*
- **Herald of Thunder** *(Lightning)*, **Herald of Ice** *(Cold)*, **Herald of Ash** *(Fire)*, **Herald of Blood** *(Physical)*, **Herald of Plague** *(Chaos)*

## Key Tag Interactions
- **Attack** — scaled by Attack Damage and Attack Speed nodes
- **Melee** — scaled by Melee Damage nodes
- **Lightning** — scaled by Lightning Damage nodes; Shocks enemies
- **Cold** — scaled by Cold Damage nodes; Chills and Freezes enemies
- **Area** — scaled by AoE and Area Damage nodes
- **Slam (Vaulting Impact)** — scaled by Slam nodes
- **Channel (Gathering Storm)** — channelling nodes apply; cannot be supported by Cast Speed
- **Combo** — Tempest Bell and Mantra of Destruction build Combo stacks that are consumed by other skills for bonus damage; scaling Combo is a core Monk mechanic

---

## Playstyle Notes
Quarterstaff is the Monk's weapon. The playstyle revolves around building and consuming Combo stacks. Tempest Flurry is the bread-and-butter damage skill. Ice Strike + Wave of Frost is a dominant Cold combo for Freeze builds. Flicker Strike requires Power Charges to teleport. The Monk's ascendancies (Invoker, Acolyte of Chayula, Martial Artist) each interact differently with Combo and elemental mechanics.
