# Crossbow

## Weapon-Skill Restriction
Skills below can **only** be used when a Crossbow is equipped. Do not suggest these skills for any other weapon type.

## Tags Reference
Tags determine what passive tree nodes and support gems affect a skill.

---

## Active Skills

### Bolts (Clip-Based)
Most bolt skills have a **Load** version and a standalone version. Load versions load a clip for sustained rapid fire; standalone versions fire immediately.

- **Explosive Shot / Load Explosive Shot** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Area · Fire)* — flaming bolt that explodes on impact; core Ignite and fire build skill
- **Fragmentation Rounds / Load Fragmentation Rounds** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Area · Physical)* — Piercing bolts that fragment in flight
- **Galvanic Shards / Load Galvanic Shards** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Lightning)* — charged bolts that release chaining Lightning beams on hit
- **Armour Piercing Rounds / Load Armour Piercing Rounds** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Physical)* — rapidly Break enemy Armour
- **High Velocity Rounds / Load High Velocity Rounds** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Physical)* — Piercing bolts that apply Riven Armour to Fully Broken Armour enemies
- **Permafrost Bolts / Load Permafrost Bolts** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Cold · Area)* — icy bolts that fragment, hitting in a cone behind the target
- **Rapid Shot / Load Rapid Shot** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Fire)* — high-speed stream of heated bolts
- **Shockburst Rounds / Load Shockburst Rounds** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Lightning · Area)* — release pulses on hitting enemies with Lightning Ailments
- **Incendiary Shot / Load Incendiary Shot** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Fire · Area)* — flaming bolts that fragment and Ignite
- **Ice Shards / Load Ice Shards** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Cold · Duration)* — icy bolts that leave armed shards at impact
- **Glacial Bolt / Load Glacial Bolt** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Cold · Area)* — icy bolt that creates two Ice Crystal walls
- **Hailstorm Rounds / Load Hailstorm Rounds** *(Attack · Ranged · Area · Cold)* — fire all bolts skyward, raining down over a target area
- **Stormblast Bolts / Load Stormblast Bolts** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Lightning · Area)* — bolts lodge in ground and explode when Detonated
- **Plasma Blast / Load Plasma Blast** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Lightning · Area · Channel)* — slow-charging devastating bolt that Pierces
- **Siege Cascade / Load Siege Cascade** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Area)* — enemy-seeking payload drops a bolt near every enemy in area

### Grenades (Cooldown-Based)
- **Explosive Grenade** *(Spell · Area · Fire · Duration)* — devastating fire blast on fuse expiry
- **Cluster Grenade** *(Spell · Area · Physical · Duration)* — explodes into a ring of mini grenades
- **Gas Grenade** *(Spell · Area · Chaos · Duration)* — creates Poison gas cloud
- **Flash Grenade** *(Spell · Area · Duration)* — Blinding and Stunning explosion
- **Voltaic Grenade** *(Spell · Area · Lightning · Duration)* — Lightning blast; contributes to Electrocution
- **Oil Grenade** *(Spell · Area · Duration)* — covers ground and enemies in Oil

### Ballista Totems
- **Siege Ballista** *(Attack · Ranged · Totem · Area)* — Ballista Totem firing bolts skyward to explode
- **Artillery Ballista** *(Attack · Ranged · Totem · Area)* — Ballista Totem raining down Pinning bolts
- **Mortar Cannon** *(Totem)* — Cannon Ballista Totem using socketed Grenade skills

### Utility
- **Emergency Reload** — instantly reload all Crossbow clips and empower them
- **Shard Scavenger** — consuming ailments on enemies reloads Crossbow and restores Grenade cooldowns
- **Requiem / Compose Requiem** *(Spell · Area · Physical)* — bombard target area with tormented projectiles

### Persistent Buffs
- **Mirage Archer** — dodge rolling creates a Mirage that fires your socketed ranged attacks
- **Wind Dancer** — periodically grants Evasion stages

### Heralds *(usable with any weapon)*
- **Herald of Thunder** *(Lightning)*, **Herald of Ice** *(Cold)*, **Herald of Ash** *(Fire)*, **Herald of Blood** *(Physical)*, **Herald of Plague** *(Chaos)*

## Key Tag Interactions
- **Attack** — scaled by Attack Damage, Attack Speed, and Accuracy nodes
- **Ranged** — scaled by Ranged Attack damage nodes
- **Projectile** — scaled by Projectile Damage nodes
- **Fire/Cold/Lightning** — determines ailment type (Ignite, Freeze, Shock)
- **Spell (Grenades)** — Grenades are Spells, NOT Attacks — scaled by Spell Damage, not Attack Damage
- **Area** — scaled by AoE and Area Damage nodes

---

## Playstyle Notes
Crossbow is the Mercenary's weapon. Bolt skills use a clip system. Grenades are cooldown-based. **Important: Grenades are Spell skills, not Attack skills** — they do not benefit from Attack Damage nodes. Armour Break is a key combo: Armour Piercing Rounds → High Velocity Rounds → burst damage.
