# Mace

## Weapon-Skill Restriction
Skills below can **only** be used when a Mace (One-Handed or Two-Handed) is equipped. Do not suggest these skills for any other weapon type.

Two-Handed Maces have access to one additional skill: **Supercharged Slam**.

## Tags Reference
Tags determine what passive tree nodes and support gems affect a skill.

---

## Active Skills

### Primary Damage
- **Boneshatter** *(Attack · Melee · Area · Physical)* — melee Strike dealing more damage based on Stun Buildup on the enemy; core Stun build skill
- **Earthquake** *(Attack · Melee · Slam · Area · Duration · Physical)* — smash the ground dealing damage in an area, leaving Jagged Ground
- **Earthshatter** *(Attack · Melee · Slam · Area · Duration · Physical)* — slam sending a fissure across the ground that thrusts a spike up at the end
- **Volcanic Fissure** *(Attack · Melee · Slam · Area · Fire · Duration)* — slam creating a branching Molten Fissure that damages as it travels
- **Sunder** *(Attack · Melee · Slam · Area · Physical)* — slam creating a roiling fissure in a sequence of areas in front of you
- **Stampede** *(Attack · Melee · Slam · Area · Physical · Travel)* — charge forward leaving Jagged Ground with every footstep
- **Rolling Slam** *(Attack · Melee · Slam · Area · Physical)* — slam to Stun and knock enemies, then continue for a second devastating Slam
- **Perfect Strike** *(Attack · Melee · Channel · Fire · Physical)* — channel to charge up your weapon with Fire before releasing
- **Molten Blast** *(Attack · Melee · Area · Fire · Physical)* — gouge molten rock from the ground and fling it
- **Forge Hammer** *(Attack · Ranged · Area · Fire · Physical)* — hurl a fiery hammer that slams into the ground
- **Supercharged Slam** *(Attack · Melee · Slam · Area · Physical)* *(Two-Handed only)* — channel a heavy swing drawing earth from the ground to enlarge your mace

### Mobility
- **Leap Slam** *(Attack · Melee · Slam · Area · Travel · Physical)* — jump into the air and land on enemies, Knocking Back

### Glory / Buff Builders
- **Hammer of the Gods** *(Attack · Melee · Slam · Area · Physical)* — build Glory by Heavy Stunning enemies; consumes Glory for a massive slam

### Totems
- **Ancestral Warrior Totem** *(Totem)* — consume 3 Endurance Charges to raise a Totem using socketed Mace skills
- **Shockwave Totem** *(Spell · Totem · Slam · Area · Physical)* — raise a Totem that repeatedly Slams the ground

### Utility
- **Armour Breaker** *(Attack · Melee · Physical)* — Strike knocking back enemies and weakening Armour

### Warcry
- **Ancestral Cry** *(Warcry · Area · Fire · Duration)* — Warcry using Glory gained from Igniting enemies; transforms into an embodiment of Kaom

### Heralds *(usable with any weapon)*
- **Herald of Thunder** *(Lightning)*, **Herald of Ice** *(Cold)*, **Herald of Ash** *(Fire)*, **Herald of Blood** *(Physical)*, **Herald of Plague** *(Chaos)*

## Key Tag Interactions
- **Attack** — scaled by Attack Damage, Attack Speed nodes
- **Melee** — scaled by Melee Damage nodes
- **Slam** — scaled by Slam damage nodes on the passive tree; many Warrior notables specifically buff Slams
- **Area** — scaled by AoE and Area Damage nodes
- **Physical** — scales with Physical Damage nodes; can be scaled with Armour Break on enemies
- **Fire** — relevant for Ignite builds (Volcanic Fissure, Molten Blast, Forge Hammer)
- **Channel** — affected by Channelling nodes (Perfect Strike)
- **Totem** — Totem skills scale with Totem-specific nodes, not the character's Attack Speed

---

## Playstyle Notes
Mace is the Warrior's primary weapon. Core mechanics are **Stun** (build Stun to amplify Boneshatter) and **Armour Break** (break Armour for massive burst windows). Slam skills (Earthquake, Earthshatter, Volcanic Fissure) deal large area damage — pair with Warbringer ascendancy for best results. Two-Handed Mace gives up a shield for higher damage and Supercharged Slam access.
