# Primal (Talisman — Druid)

## Weapon-Skill Restriction
Skills below can **only** be used when a Talisman is equipped. This is the Druid's unique weapon type.

## Tags Reference
Tags determine what passive tree nodes and support gems affect a skill.

---

## Shapeshifting System
The Druid's Talisman skills are built around shapeshifting into three animal forms. Each form has distinct skills and a distinct damage identity. You can shift between forms during combat.

---

## Active Skills

### Werewolf Form — Cold / Physical
- **Pounce** *(Attack · Melee · Area · Cold · Travel)* — shapeshift into a Werewolf and leap to a target location
- **Lunar Assault** *(Attack · Melee · Area · Cold · Physical)* — shapeshift into a Werewolf and swipe, projecting a crescent arc of moonlight and ice
- **Cross Slash** *(Attack · Melee · Area · Cold · Physical · Travel)* — shapeshift into a Werewolf and leap backwards gouging the ground
- **Arctic Howl** *(Spell · Area · Cold)* — shapeshift into a Werewolf and howl, damaging and Freezing Primed enemies
- **Lunar Blessing** *(Spell · Buff · Cold · Duration)* — offer Rage to the moon; gain Cold damage bonus for yourself and Wolf Minions

### Bear Form — Physical / Fire (Slam)
- **Ferocious Roar** *(Spell · Warcry · Area)* — shapeshift into a Bear and roar, gaining Rage and Empowering attacks to Break Armour
- **Furious Slam** *(Attack · Melee · Slam · Area · Physical)* — shapeshift into a Bear and Slam the ground with great force
- **Fury of the Mountain** *(Attack · Melee · Slam · Area · Fire)* — shapeshift into a Bear and pound the ground creating Molten Fissures
- **Rampage** *(Attack · Melee · Slam · Area · Physical · Travel)* — shapeshift into a Bear and charge forward Slamming the ground
- **Molten Crash** *(Attack · Melee · Slam · Area · Fire · Travel)* — shapeshift into a burning monstrosity and leap down, Slamming twice

### Wyvern Form — Fire / Chaos (Ranged)
- **Flame Breath** *(Spell · Area · Fire · Duration · Channel)* — shapeshift into a Wyvern and immolate enemies with a jet of fire
- **Rolling Magma** *(Spell · Projectile · Area · Fire)* — shapeshift into a Wyvern and lob a ball of magma
- **Oil Barrage** *(Spell · Projectile · Area · Chaos · Duration)* — shapeshift into a Wyvern and spit Oil at enemies
- **Wing Blast** *(Spell · Area · Physical · Travel)* — shapeshift into a Wyvern and knock back enemies with a wingbeat
- **Devour** *(Spell · Duration)* — shapeshift into a Wyvern and devour a Corpse or Cullable enemy

### Resource / Buff
- **Feral Invocation** *(Spell · Buff · Duration)* — while active, gain Energy when spending Mana
- **Walking Calamity** *(Spell · Buff)* — build Glory by gaining Rage while already at maximum Rage

### Heralds *(usable with any weapon)*
- **Herald of Thunder** *(Lightning)*, **Herald of Ice** *(Cold)*, **Herald of Ash** *(Fire)*, **Herald of Blood** *(Physical)*, **Herald of Plague** *(Chaos)*

## Key Tag Interactions
- **Attack** — Werewolf and Bear form skills are mostly Attacks; scaled by Attack Damage and Attack Speed
- **Melee** — scaled by Melee Damage nodes
- **Slam** — Bear form Slams scaled by Slam damage nodes
- **Spell** — Wyvern form skills (Flame Breath, Rolling Magma, Oil Barrage) are Spells; scaled by Spell Damage, not Attack Damage
- **Cold** — Werewolf form; Chills and Freezes
- **Fire** — Bear Slam and Wyvern form; Ignites enemies
- **Physical** — Werewolf and Bear melee hits
- **Channel** — Flame Breath is channelled; channelling nodes apply

## Form Damage Identity Summary
| Form | Damage Type | Skill Type | Ailment |
|------|------------|------------|---------|
| Werewolf | Cold + Physical | Attack (Melee) | Freeze / Chill |
| Bear | Physical + Fire | Attack (Melee · Slam) | Ignite |
| Wyvern | Fire + Chaos | Spell (Ranged) | Ignite / Poison |

---

## Playstyle Notes
Druid builds rotate between forms. Werewolf is Cold-oriented and fast. Bear is Physical/Fire Slam-oriented with Armour Break synergy. Wyvern is ranged fire/chaos and provides mobility. The Shaman and Oracle ascendancies shape which form is strongest. Note that Wyvern skills are Spells while Werewolf/Bear skills are Attacks — building for one form's damage type hurts the others.
