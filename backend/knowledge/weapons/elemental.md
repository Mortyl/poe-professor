# Elemental (Spells — Sorceress)

## Weapon Note
Elemental spells are **not weapon-restricted** — they can be cast with a Wand, Sceptre, or Staff. "Elemental" in this app refers to fire, cold, and lightning spells used primarily by the Sorceress class.

## Tags Reference
Tags determine what passive tree nodes and support gems affect a skill.

---

## Active Skills

### Fire Spells
- **Fireball** *(Spell · Projectile · Area · Fire)* — lob an explosive fire projectile dealing area damage on impact
- **Firebolt** *(Spell · Projectile · Fire)* — fire a single fast bolt of fire; good single target
- **Firestorm** *(Spell · Area · Fire · Duration)* — call down a storm of fire bolts over a target area
- **Flameblast** *(Spell · Channel · Area · Fire)* — channel to build charges, then release a devastating fire blast in a cone
- **Incinerate** *(Spell · Channel · Area · Fire)* — channel a stream of fire that grows in intensity over time
- **Flame Wall** *(Spell · Area · Fire · Duration)* — conjure a wall of fire that Ignites enemies and empowers fire projectiles passing through
- **Living Bomb** *(Spell · Duration · Fire · Area)* — apply a debuff that explodes on the target after a duration
- **Ember Fusillade** *(Spell · Projectile · Fire · Area)* — rapid fire barrage of fire bolts

### Cold Spells
- **Frostbolt** *(Spell · Projectile · Cold · Area)* — fire a slow-moving bolt of ice that can be detonated by other skills
- **Ice Nova** *(Spell · Area · Cold · Nova · Duration)* — release a nova of ice expanding outward; strong AoE Freeze setup
- **Frost Darts** *(Spell · Projectile · Cold)* — rapid fire icy darts
- **Freezing Shards** *(Spell · Projectile · Area · Cold)* — fire a cone of freezing shards
- **Eye of Winter** *(Spell · Projectile · Cold · Area)* — fire an icy spinning orb that releases shards as it travels
- **Frost Bomb** *(Spell · Area · Cold · Duration)* — frost bomb detonates after a delay, reducing enemy recovery
- **Frost Wall** *(Spell · Duration · Cold)* — conjure a wall of ice blocking enemies and projectiles
- **Icestorm** *(Spell · Area · Cold · Duration)* — call down a storm of ice crystals over a target area
- **Glacial Cascade** *(Spell · Area · Cold)* — release an icy fissure across the ground culminating in a spike
- **Comet** *(Spell · Area · Cold)* — call down a comet from the sky dealing massive area damage

### Lightning Spells
- **Arc** *(Spell · Projectile · Lightning · Chains)* — chain lightning that jumps between enemies; premier clear spell
- **Ball Lightning** *(Spell · Projectile · Area · Lightning · Duration)* — fires a slow ball of lightning pulsing damage as it travels
- **Lightning Conduit** *(Spell · Area · Lightning)* — deals massive Lightning damage to Shocked enemies, consuming the Shock; requires a Shocked target
- **Orb of Storms** *(Spell · Area · Lightning · Duration)* — stationary orb releasing lightning bolts at nearby enemies
- **Galvanic Field** *(Spell · Area · Lightning · Duration)* — release a field of lightning around a target
- **Fulmination** *(Spell · Area · Lightning)* — lightning explosion
- **Spark** *(Spell · Projectile · Lightning)* — fire sparks that travel along the ground
- **Mana Tempest** *(Spell · Area · Lightning · Duration)* — create a storm of Mana that damages enemies

### Utility / Curses
- **Conductivity** *(Spell · Curse · Lightning)* — reduces enemy Lightning Resistance
- **Flammability** *(Spell · Curse · Fire)* — reduces enemy Fire Resistance
- **Elemental Weakness** *(Spell · Curse)* — reduces all Elemental Resistances
- **Hypothermia** *(Spell · Curse · Cold)* — increases Cold damage taken by the target
- **Freezing Mark** *(Spell · Mark · Cold)* — increases Cold damage taken and makes target easier to Freeze
- **Elemental Conflux** *(Spell · Buff · Duration)* — grants all Elemental ailments on hit for a duration
- **Elemental Invocation** *(Spell · Buff)* — stores elemental instances and unleashes them

### Mobility
- **Blink** *(Spell · Travel · Duration)* — teleport to a target location instantly
- **Lightning Warp** *(Spell · Travel · Lightning · Duration)* — teleport to a target location with a short delay

### Persistent Buffs
- **Arctic Armour** *(Spell · Cold · Buff · Duration)* — grants Cold Armour while stationary
- **Discipline** *(Spell · Buff · Duration)* — reserves Spirit for bonus Energy Shield
- **Sigil of Power** *(Spell · Area · Duration)* — create a sigil granting Power Charges and bonuses in its area

## Key Tag Interactions
- **Spell** — scaled by Spell Damage, Cast Speed nodes; NOT scaled by Attack Damage
- **Projectile** — scaled by Projectile Damage and Projectile Speed nodes
- **Area** — scaled by AoE and Area Damage nodes
- **Fire** — scaled by Fire Damage nodes; Ignites enemies (DoT)
- **Cold** — scaled by Cold Damage nodes; Chills and Freezes enemies
- **Lightning** — scaled by Lightning Damage nodes; Shocks enemies (+% damage taken)
- **Channel** — channelling nodes apply; cannot be supported by Cast Speed (Flameblast, Incinerate)
- **Curse** — Curse nodes apply; enemies can have a limited number of Curses at once
- **DoT (Ignite from Fire hits)** — Ignite is a DoT scaled by Fire DoT Multiplier, not hit damage

## Critical Note: Lightning Conduit
Lightning Conduit requires an enemy to already be Shocked. It consumes the Shock on hit. Pair with Arc, Ball Lightning, or Orb of Storms to apply Shock first, then detonate with Lightning Conduit for massive burst damage.

---

## Playstyle Notes
Sorceress revolves around elemental ailments: Shock amplifies all damage taken, Ignite is DoT, Freeze stops enemies. Arc is the dominant clear spell. Lightning Conduit is a powerful single-target burst requiring a pre-Shocked enemy. Frostbolt + Ice Nova is a classic Cold combo (Ice Nova detonates Frostbolts). Comet deals enormous damage but has a cast time. Blink provides instant repositioning.
