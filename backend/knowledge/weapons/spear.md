# Spear

## Weapon-Skill Restriction
Skills below can **only** be used when a Spear is equipped. Do not suggest these skills for any other weapon type.

## Tags Reference
Tags determine what passive tree nodes and support gems affect a skill.

---

## Active Skills

### Primary Damage
- **Lightning Spear** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Area · Lightning)* — throw a single copy of your spear; core ranged skill
- **Primal Strikes** *(Attack · Melee · Lightning · Area)* — Lightning-charged stab chained into up to three attacks
- **Rapid Assault** *(Attack · Melee · Physical)* — series of six rapid stabs; high hit frequency for ailment and Leech builds
- **Spearfield** *(Attack · Melee · Area · Duration)* — stab the ground bursting multiple spears out in front of you
- **Thunderous Leap** *(Attack · Melee · Slam · Area · Lightning · Travel)* — leap and plunge your Spear creating a Lightning shockwave
- **Glacial Lance** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Cold · Area)* — throw a single Piercing lance leaving icy fragments
- **Explosive Spear** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Area · Fire · Duration)* — hurl a payload Spear that lodges in terrain then detonates
- **Storm Lance** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Lightning · Duration)* — throw an electrified Spear that periodically zaps nearby enemies
- **Fangs of Frost** *(Attack · Melee · Cold · Area)* — icy stab that exploits enemies already off balance
- **Rake** *(Attack · Melee · Area · Physical · Duration)* — dash and lacerating slash that inflicts Bleeding

### Mobility / Gap Close
- **Blood Hunt** *(Attack · Melee · Travel)* — lunge at a target and skewer them
- **Cull the Weak** *(Attack · Melee · Travel)* — dash to enemy and run them through; Culls below a Life threshold
- **Disengage** *(Attack · Melee · Area · Travel)* — jump back while rupturing the earth with spearpoints
- **Wind Serpent's Fury** *(Attack · Melee · Area · Physical)* — consume all Frenzy Charges for a gigantic spear thrust

### Utility / Setup
- **Whirling Slash** *(Attack · Melee · Area · Duration)* — circular slash creating a Whirlwind that Slows and Blinds
- **Whirlwind Lance** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile · Area · Duration)* — throw a Spear that creates a Whirlwind on landing
- **Twister** *(Attack · Melee · Area · Duration)* — whip up a Twister; area control
- **Elemental Sundering** *(Spell · Area)* — pulse that Consumes elemental ailments on nearby enemies
- **Barrage** *(Attack · Ranged · Projectile)* — empower your next Spear attack to repeat multiple times (shared with Bow)
- **Ice-Tipped Arrows** — empower next Spear attacks to convert Physical to Cold (shared with Bow)

### Persistent Buffs
- **Mirage Archer** — dodge rolling creates a Mirage that uses your socketed ranged attacks
- **Wind Dancer** — periodically grants Evasion stages

### Heralds *(usable with any weapon)*
- **Herald of Thunder** *(Lightning)*, **Herald of Ice** *(Cold)*, **Herald of Ash** *(Fire)*, **Herald of Blood** *(Physical)*, **Herald of Plague** *(Chaos)*

## Key Tag Interactions
- **Attack** — scaled by Attack Damage, Attack Speed nodes
- **Melee** — scaled by Melee Damage nodes; subject to Melee range
- **Ranged** — scaled by Ranged Attack nodes (Lightning Spear, Glacial Lance throw at range)
- **Projectile** — scaled by Projectile Damage nodes
- **Slam** — scaled by Slam damage nodes (Thunderous Leap)
- **Lightning/Cold/Fire/Physical** — determines ailments and relevant elemental scaling nodes
- **DoT (Rake/Bleed)** — Bleeding scales with Physical DoT, not hit damage nodes

---

## Playstyle Notes
Spear is the Huntress's weapon — a hybrid melee/ranged weapon. Lightning Spear is the dominant ranged skill. Primal Strikes and Rapid Assault are melee combo skills. Spear uniquely shares Barrage and Ice-Tipped Arrows with Bow. Thunderous Leap is a Slam-tagged skill, scaling with both Slam and Attack nodes.
