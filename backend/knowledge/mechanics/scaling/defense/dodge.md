# Dodge Roll

## Overview
Dodge Roll is your strongest tool to avoid damage. During the roll you are granted Invincibility Frames for roughly 80% of the animation, avoiding all Strikes and Projectiles that would otherwise hit you. When mastered, Dodge Roll dramatically reduces damage taken and the need for recovery.

---

## How Dodge Roll Works
- Grants invincibility frames for ~80% of the roll animation
- Avoids all **Strikes and Projectiles** during invincibility frames
- Does **NOT** avoid Area of Effect damage — reposition out of AoE before it lands rather than rolling through it
- No cooldown — can be used repeatedly limited only by positioning and timing

---

## Keystones

### Bulwark
- Causes Dodge Roll to reduce damage taken rather than granting invincibility frames
- **Not recommended** — removes the ability to outright avoid Strikes and Projectiles
- Turns Dodge Roll from "take zero damage" into "take less damage" — a significant downgrade in most scenarios

---

## Usage Tips
- Dodge Roll is most effective against telegraphed boss attacks — learn the animation cues
- Rolling **through** a boss rather than away puts you in a safer position behind them
- AoE ground effects cannot be rolled through — dodge to the edge of the AoE or reposition before it appears
- Many boss abilities have audio cues — keep voice lines unmuted to hear incoming attacks
- **Never stand still.** Constantly repositioning reduces incoming hit frequency even before the roll

---

## Interaction with Defences
Dodge Roll effectively reduces all other defensive requirements — a player who never gets hit does not need as much Life, Armour, or Recovery. Investing in learning Dodge Roll timing is mechanically equivalent to a large defensive upgrade.
