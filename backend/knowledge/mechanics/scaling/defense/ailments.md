# Ailments — Defensive Guide

## Overview
Ailments are negative status effects applied to the player that either deal damage over time or severely debuff your character. Bleed, Freeze, Shock, Poison, Ignite, Chill, and Stun are all extremely dangerous and should be actively countered through gear and Charms.

Charms automatically remove a specific ailment when it is applied. They use charges and do not grant full immunity — plan accordingly. Building solid defences also reduces ailment lethality by reducing the hit size that triggers them.

---

## Ailments Reference

### Bleed
- Deals **Physical Damage over Time** equal to 15% of the hit's Physical Damage dealt to Life
- Damage is **doubled while moving** — stop moving when Bleed is applied if possible
- **Counter with:** Staunching Charm, Armour (reduces the initial hit), Evasion (avoids the hit entirely)

### Freeze
- **Prevents all movement and skill use** — extremely lethal
- Applied by taking a significant Cold Hit relative to your Life pool
- **Counter with:** Thawing Charm, high Cold Resistance, "Increased Elemental Ailment Threshold" on passive tree
- The less Cold damage you take (via resistance), the harder it is to Freeze you

### Chill
- **Slows action speed** significantly
- Applied by taking Cold Damage or standing on Chilled Ground
- Annoying but rarely lethal on its own
- **Counter with:** High Cold Resistance, "Increased Elemental Ailment Threshold" on passive tree

### Shock
- Makes you **take 20% increased damage** from all sources
- Applied by taking significant Lightning Hit Damage or standing on Shocked Ground
- Often a silent killer — Shock is not always visually obvious, but dramatically amplifies the next hit
- **Counter with:** High Lightning Resistance, "Increased Elemental Ailment Threshold", Grounding Charm (lower priority than Thawing/Staunching)

### Ignite
- Deals **Fire Damage over Time** equal to 20% of the hit's Fire Damage
- **Counter with:** High Fire Resistance, "Increased Elemental Ailment Threshold", "Reduced Ignite Duration on You", Dousing Charm (lower priority than Thawing/Staunching)

### Poison
- Deals **Chaos Damage over Time**
- Life globe turns green when poisoned — use this as your visual indicator
- Multiple Poison stacks can accumulate
- **Counter with:** Chaos Resistance, Antidote Charm

### Stun
- **Prevents all actions** for a short duration
- Caused by taking too much damage too quickly relative to your Stun Threshold
- Stun Threshold is based on your Life pool by default (not Energy Shield — see `defense/energy_shield.md`)
- **Counter with:** Higher Life pool, "Increased Stun Threshold" on passive tree, "+# to Stun Threshold" on gear, better overall defences (reducing the hit size that triggers stun)

## Charm Priority
You have up to 3 Charm slots. Prioritise:
1. **Thawing Charm** (Freeze) — Freeze is the most lethal ailment. Always highest priority.
2. **Staunching Charm** (Bleed) — Bleed while moving is a common cause of unexpected death.
3. **Antidote Charm** (Poison) — Essential in Chaos-heavy areas and endgame.

Grounding (Shock) and Dousing (Ignite) Charms are lower priority — high resistances and ailment threshold investment are more efficient counters for those ailments.

---

## Ailment Threshold
"Increased Elemental Ailment Threshold" makes it harder to apply Freeze, Chill, Shock, and Ignite to you by requiring a larger hit to trigger them. Found on:
- Passive tree nodes
- Gear affixes

A cost-efficient way to reduce ailment susceptibility without using a Charm slot.
