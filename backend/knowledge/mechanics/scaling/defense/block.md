# Block

## Overview
Block gives you a chance to completely negate all incoming damage from Strikes and Projectiles, and also prevents any Ailments that hit would have applied. When a hit is Blocked, you take zero damage and suffer no ailment from that hit.

---

## How Block Works
- Applies to Strikes (melee hits) and Projectiles
- Does NOT apply to Area of Effect damage by default
- When blocked: zero damage taken, no ailment applied
- Requires a Shield equipped (primary source of Block chance)

---

## Scaling Block

**Gear**
- Shields provide base Block chance — higher quality/level shields provide more
- "% Increased Block Chance" on gear and passive tree

**Passive Tree**
- Block nodes are found near Strength starting areas and along the Warrior/Duelist border
- "Attack Block Chance" and "Spell Block Chance" are separate stats

---

## Keystones

### Glancing Blows
- **Doubles** your Chance to Block
- **Trade-off:** Blocking no longer prevents all damage — you still take a portion of the hit
- Higher Block chance but reduced per-block protection
- Worthwhile only when you have very high Block chance and want to exceed normal caps

### Turtle Charm
- Allows Block to also mitigate Area of Effect damage
- By default AoE damage cannot be Blocked — this Charm changes that

---

## Block vs Evasion
- Both provide a chance to avoid damage entirely
- Block requires a Shield; Evasion scales with Dexterity and Evasion Rating
- Block applies to Strikes AND Projectiles but not AoE
- Evasion applies to Strikes AND Projectiles but not AoE (unless Acrobatics is allocated)
- Block prevents ailments from blocked hits; Evasion prevents ailments from evaded hits

---

## Life Gain on Block
Gear can roll "+# Life gained on Block" affixes. On shield builds with high block chance this provides consistent passive recovery during combat.
