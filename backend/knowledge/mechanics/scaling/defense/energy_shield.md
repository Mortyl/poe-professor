# Energy Shield

## Overview
Energy Shield (ES) acts as a secondary health pool that sits in front of Life, absorbing incoming damage before it reaches your Life. If you haven't taken damage for a short time, Energy Shield begins to Recharge automatically.

---

## How Energy Shield Works
- Absorbs all damage types **except Bleed and Poison** before Life is affected
- Bleed and Poison deal damage directly to Life, bypassing ES entirely
- **Chaos damage removes twice as much Energy Shield** as other damage types
- Recharges automatically after a short delay without taking damage
- Grants good protection against Bleed indirectly — Bleed only triggers on hits to Life, and ES absorbs the initial hit

---

## Scaling Energy Shield

**Passive Tree**
- ES nodes near Intelligence starting areas (Witch, Sorceress positions)
- "% Increased Maximum Energy Shield" nodes and notables

**Gear**
- Base ES on Intelligence-based gear (helmets, chest, gloves, boots)
- "% Increased Energy Shield" prefix on gear
- "+# to Maximum Energy Shield" prefix on gear

## Keystones and Mechanics

### Chaos Inoculation
- Sets your maximum Life to 1
- Grants **immunity to Chaos damage**
- A worthwhile trade when heavily invested into ES, as Life becomes a negligible portion of your effective hit pool
- Life effectively becomes 1 point — all survivability comes from the ES pool
- **Almost mandatory for dedicated ES builds in the endgame**

### Mind Over Matter
- A percentage of damage taken hits Mana before Life
- Allows Mana to function as an additional defensive layer
- **Lowers Mana Recovery** — requires significant investment to counteract
- Synergises with Archmage (scale offence and defence simultaneously from Mana)
- Synergises with Eldritch Battery (allows massive Mana pool investment)
- Can be combined with Chaos Inoculation: Mana must be fully exhausted before Life can be damaged, granting effective Chaos immunity even on a 1 Life pool

### Mana Remnants
- Allows Mana to Overflow beyond its maximum
- Useful in high-Mana investment builds that use Mind Over Matter

---

## Stun and Freeze Threshold with ES
By default, Stun and Freeze thresholds are calculated from your Life pool only — Energy Shield is not factored in. This means heavy ES builds with low Life are extremely vulnerable to being stunned.

**Strongly recommended affixes for ES builds:**
- "Gain #% of Maximum Energy Shield as additional Stun Threshold"
- "Gain #% of Maximum Energy Shield as additional Freeze Threshold"
- "Stun Threshold is based on #% of your Energy Shield instead of Life"

These are near-mandatory for dedicated Energy Shield builds.

---

## Energy Shield Recovery Methods
- **Recharge** — Automatically restores ES after not taking damage for a short time. Default recovery method.
  - "Faster start of Energy Shield Recharge" — reduces the delay before Recharge begins
  - "Energy Shield Recharge Rate" — increases how quickly ES fills during Recharge
- **Regeneration** — **Zealot's Oath** (keystone) causes Life Regeneration sources to apply to ES instead. Removes ES Recharge entirely.
- **Recoup** — Recovers ES for a portion of hit damage taken, over 8 seconds.
- **Life Flasks** — **Eternal Youth** (keystone) allows Life Flasks to recover ES instead of Life.

### Overflow
- **Grim Feast** — Allows ES to Overflow up to double its maximum temporarily.

---

## When to Use Energy Shield
- Intelligence-based classes (Witch, Sorceress) naturally access ES gear and passive nodes
- High-investment builds that can reach large ES pools
- Builds using Chaos Inoculation to negate Chaos damage
- Builds using Mind Over Matter for hybrid Mana/Life defence
