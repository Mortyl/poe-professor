# Resistances

## Overview
There are 4 Resistances in PoE2: Fire, Cold, Lightning (collectively Elemental Resistances) and Chaos Resistance. Each mitigates damage taken from its respective damage type.

---

## Key Rules
- **Default cap: 75%.** Resistances cannot exceed 75% unless you have "increased Maximum Resistance" modifiers.
- **Maximum cap: 90%** with the right modifiers — a significant survivability increase.
- **Resistances start at 0%** and can go negative. Negative resistances increase damage taken.
- **Act penalties:** Each Act completion applies a -10% penalty to all resistances. By end of Act 3 Cruel, the total penalty is -40% (assuming permanent +20% resistance bonuses collected during the campaign), meaning you need 115% total elemental resistance to be capped.
- **Overcapping is useful** — sources like Flammability curse can temporarily reduce your resistances below the cap. Having 90% Fire Resistance before a curse reduces it to 75% rather than below cap.

---

## How Resistances Work
Missing resistance dramatically amplifies incoming damage. Do not think of uncapped resistance as "taking some fire damage" — think of it as "taking extra damage."

**Example:** 100 Fire Damage taken
- At 75% Fire Resistance: 25 damage taken
- At 50% Fire Resistance: 50 damage taken (double what capped takes)
- At 0% Fire Resistance: 100 damage taken
- At -25% Fire Resistance: 125 damage taken

Enemy damage in Maps is balanced around the player having 75% capped resistances. Playing with uncapped resistances is equivalent to playing on a harder difficulty.

## Resistance Targets by Act

| Stage | Target |
|-------|--------|
| Act 1 | 30%+ Elemental. Buy Resistance Rings from the Caster Vendor. Ruby Ring before Draven, Sapphire Ring(s) before Lachlann and Geonor. |
| Act 2 | Focus Fire and Lightning (main damage types in Act 2). Keep Cold positive. |
| Act 3 | Fire and Lightning remain prevalent. Keep Cold positive. |
| Acts 1–3 Cruel | Same principles. Begin approaching the resistance cap toward end of campaign. |
| Maps | All Elemental Resistances at 75%. Chaos Resistance positive (ideally capped). |

---

## Chaos Resistance
- Functions identically to elemental resistances but for Chaos damage
- Not required during the campaign but very useful
- Essential in endgame where Chaos damage sources are common
- Chaos Inoculation (keystone) sets Life to 1 but grants immunity to Chaos damage entirely — see `defense/energy_shield.md`

---

## Gear Priority for Resistances
- Prioritise resistance suffixes on rings, amulet, belt, helmet, boots, and chest
- Resistance rolls on gear always take priority over damage upgrades during the campaign
- Two resistance suffixes on a chest piece is an efficient way to cover multiple resistances at once
