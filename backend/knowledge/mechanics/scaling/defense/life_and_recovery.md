# Life and Recovery

## Overview
Life is the foundation of all defences. When Life reaches zero, you die. All other defensive layers sit on top of your Life pool — a larger pool gives every other defence more room to work.

---

## Sources of Maximum Life
- **Levelling up** — +12 Life per Character Level
- **Strength attribute** — +2 maximum Life per point of Strength
- **Gear** — "+# to Maximum Life" modifiers on any equipment slot

## PoE2 Passive Tree — Life Nodes Are Rare
**This is a critical difference from Path of Exile 1.** PoE2's passive tree has very few Maximum Life nodes. Do NOT advise players to "stack life nodes" or aim for large percentages of increased Life from the passive tree — that strategy does not exist in PoE2.

Life scaling in PoE2 comes from:
1. **Gear** — flat Maximum Life is the primary source
2. **Strength** — each 10 Strength on the tree or gear adds +20 Life
3. **Levelling** — passive +12 Life per level

When giving passive tree guidance, focus on damage notables, defensive keystones, and Strength clusters (for classes that benefit from Strength). Do not recommend life node pathing as a defensive strategy.

---

## Priority During the Campaign
Life on gear should be prioritised over damage upgrades early. A dead character deals zero damage.

- Aim for 500+ Life by Act 2, 1,000+ Life by Act 3
- Always take "+# to Maximum Life" gear upgrades when available
- Strength nodes on the passive tree contribute to Life indirectly — every 10 Strength = +20 Life

---

## Life as a Defensive Foundation
Life does not mitigate damage — it absorbs it. The effectiveness of every other defence (Armour, Evasion, Resistances, Energy Shield) scales with how much Life you have underneath it.

**Dying is a signal to check defences.** Sudden death almost always means resistances have dropped, a new damage type is being encountered, or Life pool has not kept pace with act progression.

---

## Life Recovery Methods
Life Flasks are your primary recovery tool — replace them with better bases as you progress. Additional recovery sources:

- **Regeneration** — Life ticks up slowly and consistently. Passive and gear sources.
- **Life Leech** — Restores Life over time based on a portion of damage dealt. Scales with damage output.
- **Recoup** — Recovers Life equal to a portion of hit damage taken, over 8 seconds. Effectively reduces net damage taken.
- **Life Gain on Hit** — Small recovery per hit. Very effective on fast-hitting skills.
- **Life Gain on Kill** — Recovery per kill. Strong for mapping, unreliable for bosses.
- **Life Gain on Block** — Flat or percentage recovery per blocked hit. Strong for shield builds.

### Keystones Affecting Life Recovery
- **Vaal Pact** — Life Leech becomes instant instead of over time. Disables Life Flask recovery entirely. Only take if you have significant Leech sources.
- **Oasis** — Increases Recovery from Life Flasks but disables Charms entirely. Significant trade-off — losing Freeze/Bleed immunity is usually not worth it.
- **Eternal Youth** — Swaps the behaviour of Life and Energy Shield. Life Recharges like ES, ES functions like Life.

---

## Energy Shield Interaction
Energy Shield acts as a buffer in front of Life, absorbing all damage types except Bleed and Poison before Life is affected. See `defense/energy_shield.md` for full details.
