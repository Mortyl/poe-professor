# Mana

## Overview
Mana is the primary resource used to cast skills. Most active skills cost Mana per use. Managing Mana sustain is important — running out mid-combat halts all skill usage.

---

## Mana Recovery Methods
Mana Flasks are the primary recovery tool early on — replace them with better bases as you progress.

- **Natural Regeneration** — All characters have a baseline Mana Regeneration of 4% of maximum Mana per second
- **Mana Regeneration Rate** — Scales the natural regeneration by a percentage. Found on passive tree and gear.
- **Mana Gain on Kill** — Recovers a portion of Mana per kill. Strong for clearing packs.
- **Recoup** — Recovers Mana equal to a portion of hit damage taken, over 8 seconds.
- **Mana Leech** — Restores Mana over time based on a portion of damage dealt.

### Overflow
- **Mana Remnants** — Allows Mana to Overflow up to double its maximum temporarily.

---

## Mana as a Defensive Layer

### Mind Over Matter
- A percentage of damage taken is redirected to Mana before affecting Life
- Mana effectively becomes a second Life pool
- **Lowers Mana Recovery rate** — requires significant investment in Mana Regeneration or Leech to counteract
- Synergises with Archmage (scale offence and defence from Mana simultaneously)
- Synergises with Eldritch Battery (massive Mana investment)
- Can be combined with Chaos Inoculation — Mana must be exhausted before Life is damaged, granting effective Chaos immunity

### Eldritch Battery
- Converts Energy Shield into Mana
- Allows massive Mana pool investment for Mind Over Matter or Archmage builds

---

## Mana Costs and Sustain
- Skills that hit very frequently (high attack/cast speed) drain Mana faster
- Too much investment in Hit Rate without Mana sustain causes Mana starvation
- Balance skill speed upgrades against Mana regeneration
- "Reduced Mana Cost" suffixes on gear help sustain high-frequency skills

---

## Spirit vs Mana
Spirit is a separate resource used for persistent aura-type skills (Archmage, Berserk, etc.). Spirit does not regenerate or drain — skills simply reserve a portion of it. Do not confuse Spirit costs with Mana costs.
